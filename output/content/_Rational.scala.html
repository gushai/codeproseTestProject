Rational

Rational implements rational numbers in Scala to introduce some of the basic Scala concepts and highlight codeprose.

It offers:

  • Addition (add() and +)
  • Subtraction (subtract() and -)
  • Multiplication (multiply() and *)
  • Implicit conversion of Int
  • Automatic reduction
  • And more …
